20 Complete Bike Repair Tutorials

1. 🔧 Fix a Flat Tire

Tools Needed: Tire levers, spare tube, pump, patch kit

Step 1: Remove the Wheel

Use a wrench or quick-release lever to remove the wheel from the bike.

Step 2: Remove the Tire

Use tire levers to pry the tire off the rim.

Step 3: Replace the Tube

Insert a new inner tube and partially inflate it.

Step 4: Reinstall the Tire

Fit the tire back onto the rim and inflate to recommended pressure.

2. 🚲 Adjust Your Brakes

Tools Needed: Allen wrenches, screwdriver

Step 1: Inspect Pads

Check that brake pads are aligned and not worn out.

Step 2: Adjust Levers

Use barrel adjusters to fine-tune lever tension.

Step 3: Test Brakes

Spin wheels and ensure smooth brake engagement.

3. 🛠 Lubricate Your Chain

Tools Needed: Chain lube, rag

Step 1: Clean Chain

Remove dirt and old grease with degreaser.

Step 2: Apply Lubricant

Lubricate chain while rotating pedals backward.

Step 3: Wipe Excess

Remove extra lubricant to prevent dirt accumulation.

4. 🛞 True a Wheel

Tools Needed: Spoke wrench, truing stand (optional)

Step 1: Inspect Wheel

Check for wobbles by spinning the wheel.

Step 2: Adjust Spokes

Tighten or loosen spokes to correct alignment.

Step 3: Recheck

Spin the wheel again to ensure it’s straight.

5. 🛑 Replace Brake Pads

Tools Needed: Allen wrenches, new pads

Step 1: Remove Old Pads

Unscrew old pads from caliper.

Step 2: Install New Pads

Secure new pads and ensure alignment with rim.

Step 3: Test Brakes

Spin wheel and check brake performance.

6. 🔧 Replace Inner Tube

Tools Needed: Tire levers, new tube, pump

Step 1: Remove Wheel & Tire

Use tire levers to remove the tire.

Step 2: Remove Damaged Tube

Pull out the old tube carefully.

Step 3: Insert New Tube

Place new tube inside tire and seat tire on rim.

Step 4: Inflate

Inflate tube to recommended pressure.

7. ⚙️ Adjust Gears

Tools Needed: Screwdriver, allen keys

Step 1: Inspect Derailleur

Check derailleur alignment and damage.

Step 2: Adjust Limit Screws

Set high and low limits using screwdriver.

Step 3: Fine-Tune

Use barrel adjuster to ensure smooth shifting.

8. 🧽 Clean Your Bike

Tools Needed: Brush, water, bike soap, cloth

Step 1: Rinse Frame

Use gentle water to remove dirt.

Step 2: Scrub Components

Clean chain, cassette, and derailleurs.

Step 3: Dry

Wipe all surfaces to prevent rust.

9. 🚴 Replace Pedals

Tools Needed: Pedal wrench or Allen key

Step 1: Remove Old Pedals

Turn pedals counterclockwise (right) or clockwise (left) to remove.

Step 2: Apply Grease

Grease threads before installing new pedals.

Step 3: Install New Pedals

Tighten securely without overtightening.

10. 🛠 Adjust Headset

Tools Needed: Allen keys, wrench

Step 1: Loosen Stem Bolts

Loosen stem to allow adjustment.

Step 2: Adjust Preload

Turn top cap to remove play from headset bearings.

Step 3: Tighten Bolts

Secure stem bolts and recheck alignment.
