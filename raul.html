To fix a flat tire, you'll need a pump, a tire lever, and a patch kit. Follow these steps carefully:

  1. Remove the wheel from your bike.
  2. Use the tire lever to take the tire off the rim.
  3. Find the hole in the inner tube and patch it.
  4. Put the tire back on and inflate it.

Properly adjusted brakes ensure safe riding. Here's how to fix them:

  1. Loosen the brake cable bolt.
  2. Align the brake pads with the rim.
  3. Tighten the cable again.
  4. Test the brakes to make sure they engage evenly.

If your chain breaks, follow these steps:

  1. Use a chain tool to remove the damaged link.
  2. Reattach the chain with a master link.
  3. Oil the chain to ensure smooth movement.
