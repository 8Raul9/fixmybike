20 Complete Bike Repair Tutorials

1. 🔧 Fix a Flat Tire

Tools Needed: Tire levers, spare tube, pump, patch kit

Step 1: Remove the Wheel

Use a wrench or quick-release lever to remove the wheel from the bike.

Step 2: Remove the Tire

Use tire levers to pry the tire off the rim.

Step 3: Replace the Tube

Insert a new inner tube and partially inflate it.

Step 4: Reinstall the Tire

Fit the tire back onto the rim and inflate to recommended pressure.

2. 🚲 Adjust Your Brakes

Tools Needed: Allen wrenches, screwdriver

Step 1: Inspect Pads

Check that brake pads are aligned and not worn out.

Step 2: Adjust Levers

Use barrel adjusters to fine-tune lever tension.

Step 3: Test Brakes

Spin wheels and ensure smooth brake engagement.

3. 🛠 Lubricate Your Chain

Tools Needed: Chain lube, rag

Step 1: Clean Chain

Remove dirt and old grease with degreaser.

Step 2: Apply Lubricant

Lubricate chain while rotating pedals backward.

Step 3: Wipe Excess

Remove extra lubricant to prevent dirt accumulation.

4. 🛞 True a Wheel

Tools Needed: Spoke wrench, truing stand (optional)

Step 1: Inspect Wheel

Check for wobbles by spinning the wheel.

Step 2: Adjust Spokes

Tighten or loosen spokes to correct alignment.

Step 3: Recheck

Spin the wheel again to ensure it’s straight.

5. 🛑 Replace Brake Pads

Tools Needed: Allen wrenches, new pads

Step 1: Remove Old Pads

Unscrew old pads from caliper.

Step 2: Install New Pads

Secure new pads and ensure alignment with rim.

Step 3: Test Brakes

Spin wheel and check brake performance.

6. 🔧 Replace Inner Tube

Tools Needed: Tire levers, new tube, pump

Step 1: Remove Wheel & Tire

Use tire levers to remove the tire.

Step 2: Remove Damaged Tube

Pull out the old tube carefully.

Step 3: Insert New Tube

Place new tube inside tire and seat tire on rim.

Step 4: Inflate

Inflate tube to recommended pressure.

7. ⚙️ Adjust Gears

Tools Needed: Screwdriver, allen keys

Step 1: Inspect Derailleur

Check derailleur alignment and damage.

Step 2: Adjust Limit Screws

Set high and low limits using screwdriver.

Step 3: Fine-Tune

Use barrel adjuster to ensure smooth shifting.

8. 🧽 Clean Your Bike

Tools Needed: Brush, water, bike soap, cloth

Step 1: Rinse Frame

Use gentle water to remove dirt.

Step 2: Scrub Components

Clean chain, cassette, and derailleurs.

Step 3: Dry

Wipe all surfaces to prevent rust.

9. 🚴 Replace Pedals

Tools Needed: Pedal wrench or Allen key

Step 1: Remove Old Pedals

Turn pedals counterclockwise (right) or clockwise (left) to remove.

Step 2: Apply Grease

Grease threads before installing new pedals.

Step 3: Install New Pedals

Tighten securely without overtightening.

10. 🛠 Adjust Headset

Tools Needed: Allen keys, wrench

Step 1: Loosen Stem Bolts

Loosen stem to allow adjustment.

Step 2: Adjust Preload

Turn top cap to remove play from headset bearings.

Step 3: Tighten Bolts

Secure stem bolts and recheck alignment.

11. 🪑 Replace Saddle

Tools Needed: Allen keys

Step 1: Loosen Clamp

Loosen the saddle clamp bolt.

Step 2: Remove Old Saddle

Slide off the old saddle from seatpost.

Step 3: Install New Saddle

Position and tighten new saddle at desired angle.

12. ✋ Replace Handlebar Grips

Tools Needed: Alcohol or compressed air

Step 1: Remove Old Grips

Twist and pull off grips; use alcohol if stuck.

Step 2: Install New Grips

Slide new grips onto handlebars, let dry if using alcohol.

13. 🔧 Adjust Suspension

Tools Needed: Suspension tools or manual adjustment

Step 1: Set Sag

Adjust preload for rider weight.

Step 2: Adjust Rebound

Turn rebound knob to control speed of compression release.

Step 3: Adjust Compression

Set compression to preference for terrain.

14. 🔗 Replace Brake/Shift Cables

Tools Needed: Cable cutters, new cables

Step 1: Remove Old Cable

Loosen bolt and pull cable from housing.

Step 2: Insert New Cable

Feed cable through housing and attach to components.

Step 3: Adjust Tension

Use barrel adjuster to fine-tune cable tension.

15. 🪑 Adjust Seat Height

Tools Needed: Allen key

Step 1: Loosen Seatpost Clamp

Loosen bolt to move seat.

Step 2: Set Height

Position seat so leg extends slightly when pedal is bottomed.

Step 3: Tighten Clamp

Secure seat in place.

16. ⛓ Replace Chain

Tools Needed: Chain tool, new chain

Step 1: Remove Old Chain

Use chain tool to break chain.

Step 2: Fit New Chain

Measure length and connect new chain using pin or master link.

Step 3: Lubricate

Apply chain lubricant for smooth operation.

17. 🎈 Check Tire Pressure

Tools Needed: Tire pressure gauge, pump

Step 1: Measure Pressure

Use gauge to check PSI against recommended pressure.

Step 2: Inflate/Deflate

Add or release air to reach proper PSI.

18. ⚙️ Replace Pedal Bearings

Tools Needed: Allen key, wrench, grease

Step 1: Remove Pedal

Unscrew pedal from crank arm.

Step 2: Open Pedal

Remove end cap and bearings.

Step 3: Reassemble

Grease new bearings and reassemble pedal.

19. 🔧 Replace Bottom Bracket

Tools Needed: Bottom bracket tool, wrench, grease

Step 1: Remove Crankset

Use crank puller to remove cranks.

Step 2: Remove Old BB

Unscrew bottom bracket using BB tool.

Step 3: Install New BB

Grease threads and screw in new bottom bracket.

20. ✅ General Bike Safety Check

Tools Needed: None

Step 1: Check Brakes

Ensure all brakes work properly.

Step 2: Check Tires

Inspect for wear, punctures, and proper PSI.

Step 3: Inspect Chain & Gears

Check for wear, smooth shifting, and lubrication.

Step 4: Check Bolts & Components

Ensure all bolts, handlebars, pedals, and seat are secure.
